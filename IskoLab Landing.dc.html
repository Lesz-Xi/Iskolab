FAQ · May tanong ka?

Sagot muna, bago ka mag-start.

01Libre ba ang IskoLab?Hover

Oo — free to use para sa exam prep mo. Set an exam plan, generate study packs, and practice without paying. Walang hidden paywall sa gitna ng review mo.

02Anong subjects ang gagana rito?Hover

Kahit ano — from PI 100 and Philippine History to Biology, Accounting, or Nursing. IskoLab doesn’t ship its own content; it structures your notes and modules, so it works for whatever you’re actually enrolled in.

03English lang ba, o pwedeng Taglish?Hover

English, Taglish, o Filipino — ikaw ang pipili. The explanations and reviewers adapt to how you actually think, kaya mas madaling maintindihan, hindi puro textbook-speak.

04Paano kung PDF lang ang module ko?Hover

Upload the PDF and IskoLab pulls the text out for you. Kung scanned images ang laman at hindi mabasa, i-paste mo na lang ang teksto — same result, walang hassle.

05Kailangan ko bang mag-download?Hover

Wala. It runs in any phone browser — walang app store, walang storage na uubusin. Gusto mo parang app? Add to Home Screen and it opens full-screen like one.

06Safe ba ang notes at material ko?Hover

Ang material na ini-add mo ay ginagamit lang para buuin ang study pack mo — that’s it. It’s tied to your own review, hindi ibinebenta o ipinapamigay.
